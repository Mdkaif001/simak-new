    We Deliver Transformational GeoSpatial

    Simak IT is a technology system integrator in geospatial technology offering end-to-end solutions that empower industries to make data-driven decisions with precision. By leveraging advanced technologies like AI, Machine Learning, IoT, Biometrics, and Blockchain—alongside LiDAR, GPR, Drones, Satellite Imagery, and BIM—NeoGeo delivers actionable geospatial insights that drive smarter outcomes.


    Simak IT is Engaged In:

     Simak IT specializes in providing comprehensive geospatial solutions across a variety of sectors, including Utilities, Urban Governance, Infrastructure, and Natural Resources Management.


    Our Core Services Include:
    • System Integration:
      Implementing cutting-edge geospatial technologies seamlessly into existing workflows across Urban, Utility Networks, and Natural Resource Management verticals.
    • Consulting & Analytics:
      Transforming spatial data into strategic insights through powerful analytics platforms for Urban Planning, Development, and Management.
    • Asset Management:
      Delivering customized asset management solutions for utilities and large-scale infrastructure projects.
    • Geospatial Data Collection & Analysis:
      Utilizing state-of-the-art tools such as LiDAR and drones to gather and interpret geospatial data.
    • Mapping and Data Acquisition Services: Accurate and Reliable Data Acquisition is the foundation of effective geospatial solutions. Simak IT offers a comprehensive suite of mapping and data acquisition services designed to meet diverse project requirements. Leveraging cutting-edge technologies, our solutions empower organizations with precise spatial data for informed decision-making and optimized operations.
    • High-Resolution Aerial and Satellite Mapping: Capture detailed imagery from satellites and aircraft, providing comprehensive spatial coverage for infrastructure planning, urban development, and environmental monitoring.
    • LiDAR Surveys (Airborne, Terrestrial, Mobile): Use LiDAR technology to generate high-precision elevation data, suitable for terrain modeling, asset mapping, and risk assessment.
    • UAV/Drone-Based Surveys: Deploy drones for rapid and cost-effective data collection, enabling site inspections, volumetric analysis, mapping inaccessible areas and land information management.
    • Photogrammetry and 3D Mapping: Transform 2D imagery into accurate 3D models for land surveys, construction planning, and heritage site preservation.
    • GPR Surveys: Employ Ground Penetrating Radar for subsurface exploration, ideal for utilities mapping, archaeological studies, and geotechnical investigations.
    • 360-Degree Video and Attribute Surveys: Combine immersive visuals with attribute data to enhance asset management, road condition assessments, and public infrastructure planning.
